WE ARE GDSC-CIT

The Google Club at our college is a community for tech enthusiasts passionate about innovation, collaboration, and learning. We host workshops, seminars, hackathons, and quizzes to build skills in programming, AI, web development, and more. Our mission is to empower students, foster creativity, and bridge the gap between technology and education.
